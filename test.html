Researchers from Emory University are conducting a study to improve our understanding of the current environmental health practices of healthcare providers in the United States.

They are seeking to recruit U.S. healthcare providers (physicians, physician assistants, and advanced practice nurses) specializing in primary care or the following specialties: family medicine, internal medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics-gynecology, or women's health.

Participants will be prompted to complete a survey which will inquire about knowledge, beliefs, and attitudes surrounding environmental health topics. The survey will be administered online to participants and will take approximately 20 minutes to complete.

Monetary incentives are offered for randomly selected participants!
